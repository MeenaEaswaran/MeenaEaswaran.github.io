Original Work

Evaluation of mouse laryngeal inflammation upon low and high dose cigarette smoke exposures via NanoString analysis

This study is part of my professional work at Stanford University. It investigates the effects of low- and high-dose cigarette smoke exposures in murine larynges using the NanoString nCounter inflammation panel to evaluate inflammation and immune responses.

Key Learnings

NanoString Gene Expression Data Analysis & Visualization with R programming
QIAGEN Ingenuity Pathway Analysis

Academic Projects

Calpain10 (CAPN10): Unraveling its Significance in Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) and Insulin Resistance

This academic project aims to examine the role of the CAPN10-Calpain 10 gene, a significant player in insulin resistance and Type 2 Diabetes, in PCOS, as part of the UTHealthBMI 5330: Introduction to Bioinformatics course.

Key Learnings

CAPN10 Expression in Human Tissues
Tools: Human Protein Atlas (HPA), GTEx Portal, EMBL-EBI Expression Atlas.
Inter-Species Conservation of CAPN10
Tools: UCSC Genome Browser.
CAPN10 Genetic Variations
Tools: NCBI dBSNP, dbVAR, ClinVaR.
NCBI GEO Analysis with R Bioconductor
Tools: GEOquery and limma packages.
Functional Enrichment Analysis
Tools: PCOS Knowledge Base (PCOSKB) inbuilt functional enrichment analysis.
